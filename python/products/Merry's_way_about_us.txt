Welcome to Moonflour and Bean, your neighborhood coffee shop located in the heart of Westlands, Nairobi. At Moonflour and Bean, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience, a moment of joy, and a way to connect with others.

Our Story
Founded in 2024, Moonflour and Bean started as a small family-owned café with one mission: to share the love of quality, ethically-sourced coffee with our community. We ensure that every cup we brew tells a story of dedication and care, from farm to table. Our beans are roasted in-house to bring out unique flavors that reflect the regions where they were grown.

Delivery & Locations Served
In addition to offering a cozy place to enjoy coffee in our café, we proudly deliver across Nairobi, ensuring you never miss your daily cup of coffee. Whether you’re at home, in the office, or enjoying a day at Uhuru Park, we bring your favorite coffee right to your door. Just a click away, our delivery service ensures that your coffee moments are always within reach.

Our Menu
Our menu offers something for everyone, from our signature espresso blends to refreshing cold brews, artisanal teas, and fresh-baked goods sourced from local bakeries. Don’t miss our best-selling almond croissants or signature cappuccinos—crafted to perfection. We also cater to a variety of dietary needs with a range of plant-based milk options and gluten-free snacks.

Community & Sustainability
At Moonflour and Bean, we are more than just coffee. We are part of the community, and we care deeply about sustainability. We use eco-friendly packaging, partner with small-scale local farmers, and strive to minimize our carbon footprint. Our café regularly hosts events, such as live music nights, art showcases, and community fundraisers, making it a hub for creativity and connection.
